## Overview
This project presents a **Financial KPI Dashboard built in Excel**, designed to help leadership quickly assess performance and support **data-driven business decisions**.

The focus of this project is not just visualization, but **structuring data around meaningful business questions**.
---

## Business Questions Addressed
- Where are expenses increasing versus improving efficiency?
- Do cost increases align with growth initiatives?
- How is sales performance tracking against plan?
- Are customer satisfaction levels improving year over year?
- Does receivables aging indicate any cash-flow risk?
- How do hiring and termination trends impact overall costs?

---

## Key Insights
- **Expense growth is targeted and intentional**
- **Higher commercial and operational spend aligns with growth initiatives**
- **Overall costs remain well controlled, alongside improved customer satisfaction**
