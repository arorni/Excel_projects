This excel data analysis project deals with the analysis of sales by a fictitious store named as Vrinda store. 
With the help of the dashboard, one can easily get below insights about Vrinda stores:

- Month with the peak sales and the highest no. of orders.
- Top five states in terms of sales.
- Which platform is commonly used by the Vrinda's customers.
- Who are the main customers for Vrinda in terms of Sex and Age.
- How well Vrinda is serving its customers based on the order status.
